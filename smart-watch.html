Waterproof Smartwatch with Heart Rate K12 - IP68, Bluetooth 4.0, iOS 8.0+, Android 4.4+

Live a healthier life, and keep your top form! The K12 smartwatch is an ideal all-day and night wearing smart gadget. A super-slim metal housing with a silicone strap makes it great for elegant as well as sporty occasions.

This waterproof smartwatch K12 tracks and informs you about your daily activities, sleeping quality, calls, SMS, and social media notifications. The K12 smartwatch allows you to check blood pressure, heart rate, and blood oxygen. The smartwatch K12 features eight motion modes: walk, run, swim, cycle, basketball, badminton, football, and rope skip.

Features: - The K12 is a combination of a smart-band and smartwatch - It follows you anywhere, even in the water - IP68 rating - The K12 smartwatch is designed with eight different motion modes - It also registers the steps, burned calories, and distance - This smartwatch will become your great personal assistant - Download the corresponding app on your phone and enjoy a lot of useful functions - You can use it also as a music remote, alarm clock, reminders, stopwatch, etc.
